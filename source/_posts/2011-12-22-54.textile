<ol>
	<li>导出个人pfx证书，注意要带上证书(windows下可使用jailbreak)</li>
	<li>使用openssl将pfx转换成pem格式 
[cc lang="php" tab_size="2" lines="40"]
openssl pkcs12 -in alibaba.pfx -out alibaba.pem -nodes
[/cc]</li>
	<li>curl or wget
</ol>

[cc lang="php" lines="40"]
wget --certificate=alibaba.pem --certificate-type=pem --private-key=alibaba.pem --private-key-type=pem --ca-certificate=alibaba.pem --no-check-certificate www.cn.alibaba-inc.com

curl -s -k --cert alibaba.pem -L https://www.cn.alibaba-inc.com
[/cc]
</li>
